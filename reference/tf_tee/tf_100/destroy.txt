Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.0.0.201003261741)

 destroy command:

  Destroys, or permanently deletes, version-controlled items from Team Foundation version control.

 Valid option sets:

  destroy [-keephistory] [-startcleanup] [-preview] [-silent] [-stopat:<value>] [-force]
          itemspec1[;versionspec][;XdeletionID] [itemspec2...itemspecN]
