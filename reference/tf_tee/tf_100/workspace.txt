Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.0.0.201003261741)

 workspace command:

  Enables you to create, delete, and modify properties and mappings associated with a workspace.

 Valid option sets:

  workspace [-collection:<url>] [-comment:<value>|@valuefile] [-newname:<value>] [<workspacename;[workspaceowner]>]
  workspace -delete [-collection:<url>] [<workspacename;[workspaceowner]>]
  workspace -new [-noprompt] [-template:<value>] [-computer:<value>] [-comment:<value>|@valuefile]
            [-collection:<url>] [<workspacename;[workspaceowner]>]
