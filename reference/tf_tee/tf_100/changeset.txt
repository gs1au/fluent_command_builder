Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.0.0.201003261741)

 changeset command:

  Displays information about and lets you change the attributes, such as comments and check-in notes, that are
  associated with a changeset.

 Valid option sets:

  changeset [-latest] [-comment:<value>|@valuefile] [-notes:"note"="value"[;"note2"="value2"[;...]]|@notefile]
            [changenumber]
