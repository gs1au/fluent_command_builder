Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.0.0.201003261741)

 resolve command:

  Lets you resolve conflicts between changed items in your workspace and the latest or destination versions of
  items on the server.

 Valid option sets:

  resolve [-auto:AcceptMerge|AcceptTheirs|AcceptYours|OverwriteLocal|DeleteConflict|AcceptYoursRenameTheirs]
          [-preview] [-converttotype:<value>] [-recursive] [-newname:<value>] <itemSpec>
  resolve [-auto:AcceptMerge|AcceptTheirs|AcceptYours|OverwriteLocal|DeleteConflict|AcceptYoursRenameTheirs]
          [-preview] [-overridetype:<value>] [-recursive] [-newname:<value>] <itemSpec>
