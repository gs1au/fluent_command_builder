Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.1.0.201101271439)

 labels command:

  Displays the list of labels in the source control server, optionally filtering the results with the given label name and scope.  The filter is given as
  <labelName> or <labelName>@<scope> and may include wildcards.

 Valid option sets:

  labels [-owner:<value>] [-format:brief|detailed|xml] [<labelNameFilter>]
