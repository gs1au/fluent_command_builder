Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.1.0.201101271439)

 online command:

  When disconnected from the network, you may edit files without pending changes to the server.  The online command will synchronize your local folders with
  the server to pend edits, adds and deletes to files you have changed.
  
  You should not rename files when offline.

 Valid option sets:

  online [-adds] [-deletes] [-diff] [-exclude:<value>[,<value>]] [-recursive] [-preview] [<itemSpec>...]
