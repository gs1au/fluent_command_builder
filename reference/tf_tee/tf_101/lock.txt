Team Explorer Everywhere (version 10.1.0.201101271439)

 lock command:

  Changes the lock level of one or more files or folders.  Locks deny other users the right to check out or check in changes to a file from another workspace.
  A lock level of "none" permits changes by other users.

 Valid option sets:

  lock [-recursive] [-lock:none|checkin|checkout] <itemSpec>...
